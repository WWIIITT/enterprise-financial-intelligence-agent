# Client Communication Policy

## Policy Owner

Compliance and Client Communications Review.

## Purpose

This policy defines requirements for communications sent to clients, prospects, external partners, regulators, or the public when content is drafted, summarized, or assisted by AI systems.

## Approved Communication Uses

Approved AI systems may assist with:

- Drafting internal outlines.
- Summarizing public financial documents.
- Preparing first drafts of operational updates.
- Translating approved content for internal review.
- Generating FAQ drafts from approved knowledge-base material.

All external communication requires human review before release.

## Prohibited Communication Uses

Users must not use AI systems to:

- Send client communications without approval.
- Personalize investment recommendations without suitability review.
- Include confidential client data in prompts to unapproved tools.
- Omit required disclaimers or risk language.
- Present generated content as verified fact without source review.

## Required Disclosures and Review

Client-facing material must be reviewed for accuracy, balance, clarity, risk disclosure, and consistency with approved messaging. If AI assistance was used, reviewers must verify citations and remove unsupported statements.

## Escalation

Escalate to Compliance when communication involves performance claims, forecasts, investment recommendations, regulatory matters, complaints, or potential disclosure of confidential information.
